About Me

I am a doctoral student in Engineering Education at FIU, working under Dr. David Delaine in the iCBL Lab. My dissertation centers on the FORGE Reciprocal Partnership model, which applies Fetterman's Empowerment Evaluation to community-academic partnerships as developmental contexts for engineering professional formation. I have a background in electronics, embedded systems, and IoT development, and have been tutoring since 2016. I organized a Secondary School Girls Robotics Summer Camp that became a formative experience shaping my equity-centered teaching philosophy.
